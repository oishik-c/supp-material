David has a scheduled meeting with his team. He arrives late, entering the room while the discussion is already in progress. His colleague, Anil, notices him quietly taking a seat at the back. Seeing David join late and appear slightly unprepared, Anil reflects on how it disrupts focus and gives a careless impression. He makes a mental note to always be on time unlike David, believing it shows respect for the team and helps him stay fully engaged from the start.
