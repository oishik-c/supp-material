Karan walks up to a long queue at a ticket counter and quietly slips into a spot near the front, skipping several people. Those nearby notice but remain silent, choosing not to confront him. A short distance away, Meera observes the situation unfold. After a moment, she steps forward and addresses Karan, firmly telling him that cutting the line is unfair to everyone waiting. Then she turns to the silent crowd, pointing out that staying quiet in the face of unfairness allows it to continue. Her words leave both Karan and the others quietly reflective.
