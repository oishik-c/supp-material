Ravi is seated on a crowded bus when an elderly man named Mr. Das boards and stands nearby. Ravi notices but does not offer his seat. Among the standing passengers, a young man named Arjun sees the situation. Watching Mr. Das hold the rail with some effort, Arjun disapproves of the actions of Mr. Das. He decides silently that, in the future, he will always offer his seat to someone in need. The bus continues on, the moment leaving a lasting impression on him.
